Questions

Frequently asked

Are the calls real?

Yes. MNB Omni Caller places genuine phone calls through enterprise voice infrastructure — the same technology that powered the demo. Agents speak, listen and respond live.

Do I need any technical skills?

None. Everything is done from a simple dashboard — train the agent, place a call, read the transcript. If you can use a web form, you can use this.

Can I give each client their own access?

Absolutely. That's the core of the platform. You approve each organization and delegate specific agents, phone numbers and monthly minute limits. Clients only ever see their own data.

What languages are supported?

90+ languages with 1000+ voices, including Hindi and English (India). Agents can even switch languages mid-conversation.

How do I get started?

Click “Request access”, tell us your organization, and MNB Research will approve you and set up your agents. You'll be placing calls the same day.
